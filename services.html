Our Services

SnapFix SA offers a dynamic range of services designed to make public infrastructure management more responsive, transparent, and community-driven. Through our web and mobile platform, users can easily report infrastructure issues using photos, voice recordings, or text. Each report is geo-tagged and sent directly to the appropriate municipal department for swift action.



For local governments and organizations, we provide real-time analytics, task assignment tools, and status tracking to help manage and resolve issues efficiently. Our premium features include inspection checklists, work order management, heatmaps, and downloadable reports for deeper insights and improved service delivery. With community features like a points-based reward system, leaderboards, and crowdsourced funding tools, we foster active citizenship while supporting smarter cities.
