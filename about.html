Vision, Mission & Purpose

Vision: To become the leading platform for civic engagement and transparent communication between communities and municipal governments in South Africa.

Mission: To empower citizens with simple, accessible tools to report community problems, promote accountability, and drive timely responses from local authorities.

Purpose: We exist to ensure that every community member has a voice and a means to take action when facing public infrastructure challenges. By making issue reporting easy and transparent, we aim to create safer, more responsive, and more connected neighborhoods.
